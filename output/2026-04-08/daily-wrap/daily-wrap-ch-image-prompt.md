# Daily Wrap Card — Calvin & Hobbes Illustration Prompt

Paste the block below into ChatGPT-4o image generation, DALL-E 3, or Midjourney. Landscape orientation, roughly 3:2 aspect ratio.

For the base style reference, see [triplex/prompts/ch_image_style_guide.md](../../../triplex/prompts/ch_image_style_guide.md).

---

## Prompt

Illustrate in the style of Bill Watterson's Calvin and Hobbes. Hand-drawn ink outlines with visible pen strokes, soft watercolor fills with slight edge bleed, off-white cream paper background with a light watercolor wash texture. Flat watercolor coloring with minimal shading, just enough to give volume. No photographic realism, no digital gradients, no 3D effects, no drop shadows, no corporate logos.

Scene: Our recurring young adult character at his wooden office desk, caught in a full-body celebration. He has messy short brown hair, wears a white long-sleeve dress shirt with sleeves rolled up, a bright emerald green tie that is flung backward over his shoulder from the sudden movement, and dark trousers. His head is tilted back, mouth wide open in a roar of joy, eyes squeezed shut, both fists raised high overhead. He is mid-yell, mid-jump, pure unfiltered euphoria.

Around the character: five or six sheets of white printer paper caught in midair, swirling around him as if a gust of wind or pure energy has blown them off the desk. The flying papers give the scene motion and the classic Watterson "papers erupting into celebration" energy.

On the desk beside him: a computer monitor showing a huge bright green ascending arrow climbing steeply from lower-left to upper-right, with the plain text "DOW +1,312" visible near the arrow. Next to the monitor is a flip-style paper desk calendar, the top page showing "APRIL 8" in bold hand-drawn letters with a red circle drawn around the date.

On the floor at the base of the desk: a wooden oil barrel tipped over on its side, dark crude oil spilling out into a puddle across the floorboards. This is the oil crash that drove the equity rally.

In the upper-left corner of the frame: a small square office window looking outside. Through the window, a stylized white peace dove silhouetted in flight against a warm orange-and-gold sunrise sky. This represents the US-Iran ceasefire announcement that triggered the rally. The window frame is simple dark wood.

A ceramic coffee mug on the desk has been jostled by the celebration — two or three drops of coffee are frozen mid-splash in the air above it.

Soft watercolor palette: cream paper background, white shirt, bright emerald green tie as the strongest character color accent, messy brown hair, warm wooden desk and floorboards, vivid green monitor chart, orange-and-gold sunrise through the window as the second color anchor, dark brown oil spill on the floor, scattered white papers catching light. Visible ink outlines throughout, slightly uneven and hand-drawn.

Landscape aspect ratio, roughly 3:2 or 16:10. The character is centered in the frame with papers swirling around him. The monitor and calendar sit on the left third of the desk in the foreground. The window with the peace dove and sunrise is in the upper-left corner of the frame. The tipped oil barrel is at the bottom-right of the desk in the lower-right of the frame.

Mood: unfiltered market euphoria. Best day for the Dow since April 2025. A historic rally driven by an oil crash and a geopolitical ceasefire. The viewer should feel the energy through the character's body language and the paper storm alone — the props explain why.

Hard rules:
- No photographic realism
- No digital gradients, no drop shadows, no 3D rendering
- No corporate logos, no real news outlet branding
- No text on the image except "DOW +1,312" on the monitor and "APRIL 8" on the calendar
- Character must feel hand-drawn with watercolor, not vector art
- The peace dove should be a simple white silhouette with wings spread, clearly a dove, against a warm sunrise gradient sky
- The flying papers should be plain white sheets, tumbling and spinning, not neatly folded documents

## Post-specific notes

- Tie color: BRIGHT EMERALD GREEN (rally day)
- Key props: monitor with "DOW +1,312" green arrow, wall calendar "APRIL 8" circled, flying papers, tipped oil barrel, peace dove window, coffee mug mid-splash

- Character expression: head back, mouth open in a roar of joy, eyes squeezed shut, fists raised

## Regeneration notes

- If the oil barrel is missing, add: "A wooden or steel oil barrel MUST be tipped over on its side at the base of the desk with dark crude oil spilling out. This is the key story prop."
- If the peace dove window is unclear, add: "In the upper-left corner of the frame, a small square window with a simple dark wood frame. Through the window, a stylized white dove in flight with wings spread, silhouetted against an orange-and-gold sunrise. The dove is a clear recognizable peace dove, not a seagull."
- If the papers look too neat, add: "The flying papers are plain white printer sheets, caught mid-air, tumbling and rotating chaotically. At least five sheets visible."
- If the calendar is missing, add: "On the desk, a flip-style paper desk calendar with the top page showing 'APRIL 8' in bold hand-drawn letters, a red circle drawn around the date with a marker."
