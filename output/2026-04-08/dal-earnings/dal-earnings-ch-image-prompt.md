## Scene prompt

Young adult man in his late 20s, messy short brown hair, clean-shaven, expressive eyebrows. He wears a white long-sleeve dress shirt with the sleeves rolled up to mid-forearm, a navy blue tie loose around his neck, and dark trousers. He is standing beside his wooden office desk with both arms raised high overhead in a victorious celebration pose, fists clenched. His face shows a huge open-mouthed grin, eyes squeezed shut in pure joy. His posture is energetic, as if he just heard the best news of the morning.

On the desk to his left: a computer monitor displaying a bright green ascending stock chart line climbing steeply from lower-left to upper-right, with the plain text "DAL" in the corner of the screen. Next to the monitor sits a simple white paper airplane, a nod to Delta's airline business. A ceramic coffee mug with a wisp of steam curling up sits beside the monitor.

On the floor behind him: a wooden oil barrel tipped over on its side, with a small puddle of dark liquid (crude oil) spreading out and two or three droplets frozen mid-flight as if the barrel just fell. This represents the Iran ceasefire crashing oil prices the same morning Delta reported, two tailwinds at once.

Landscape aspect ratio, roughly 3:2 or 16:10. The character fills about 40% of the frame height, positioned slightly right of center. The monitor and paper airplane occupy the left third. The tipped oil barrel is in the lower-right corner of the frame, behind and to the right of the character.

Mood: pure celebration. Two tailwinds at once, a clean Q1 earnings beat and a 17% oil crash from the ceasefire. The viewer should immediately feel the double-win morning energy through the character's body language alone.

Hard rules:
- No real Delta Air Lines logo. The monitor shows only the plain letters "DAL"
- No text on the image except "DAL" on the monitor
- No photographic realism
- No stock photography elements
- The oil barrel must be clearly identifiable as an oil drum (ridged metal cylinder), tipped on its side with visible spill
