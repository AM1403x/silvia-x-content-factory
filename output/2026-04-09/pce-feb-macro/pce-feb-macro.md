𝗣𝗖𝗘 FEB HOT. Headline +2.8% vs 2.6% consensus. Core +3.0% vs 2.9%. Both the monthly core and headline printed +0.4%. Personal income fell 0.1% while spending rose 0.5%. The Fed's favorite inflation gauge just validated yesterday's hawkish hold.

The miss was small in absolute terms but large in direction. Both headline and core came in above consensus, and the monthly pace accelerated from January's +0.3% headline to February's +0.4%. Core held at +0.4% month over month. Nothing in this report says inflation is cooling.

That math only works because the saving rate compressed to 4.0%. Americans are spending what they don't have, which is not sustainable past a few months.
